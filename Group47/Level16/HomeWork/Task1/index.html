Image Section

Lionel Messi celebrating after scoring a goal for FC Barcelona.

Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, is often hailed as one of the greatest footballers. Known for his extraordinary dribbling, precise passing, and lethal goal-scoring, Messi has broken numerous records and won multiple awards, including the prestigious Ballon d'Or. His journey from the youth teams of Newell's Old Boys to stardom at FC Barcelona and beyond is a testament to his dedication and love for the game.

Info Section

  • Full Name: Lionel Andrés Messi
  • Date of Birth: June 24, 1987
  • Place of Birth: Rosario, Argentina
  • Height: 1.70 m (5 ft 7 in)
  • Position: Forward
  • Current Club: Inter Miami (as of 2023)
  • Previous Clubs: FC Barcelona (2000-2021), Paris Saint-Germain (2021-2023)
  • National Team: Argentina (2005-present)
  • Achievements:
    • 7x Ballon d'Or winner
    • 4x Champions League winner
    • Multiple La Liga titles
    • Copa América champion (2021)
    • FIFA World Cup champion (2022)
  • Playing Style: Messi is known for his exceptional dribbling, vision, and ability to score from various positions. His low center of gravity and quick acceleration make him a nightmare for defenders.
