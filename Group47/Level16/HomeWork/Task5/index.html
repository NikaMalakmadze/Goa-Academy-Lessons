Image Section

The Samsung logo.

Samsung is a South Korean multinational conglomerate known for its diverse range of products and services. It is one of the world's largest manufacturers of smartphones, televisions, and home appliances, with a strong emphasis on research and development.

Info Section

  • Title:About Samsung
  • Founded:March 1, 1938
  • Founders:Lee Byung-chul
  • Headquarters:Suwon, South Korea
  • Key Products:
    • Smartphones:Galaxy S and Galaxy Note series are industry leaders.
    • Tablets:Galaxy Tab series offers versatility and performance.
    • Televisions:Known for QLED and OLED technology.
    • Home Appliances:Includes refrigerators, washing machines, and more.
    • Wearables:Galaxy Watch and Galaxy Buds lead in the wearable market.
  • Services:
    • Samsung Pay: Mobile payment and digital wallet service.
    • Samsung SmartThings: Smart home platform for device control and automation.
    • Samsung Health: Health and fitness tracking app.
  • Core Values:
    • Innovation: Pioneering new technologies and product designs.
    • Quality: Commitment to high standards and reliability.
    • Sustainability: Focus on eco-friendly practices and corporate responsibility.
