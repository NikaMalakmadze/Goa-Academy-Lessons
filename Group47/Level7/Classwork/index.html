About Pizza

Pizza is a beloved dish that varies widely around the world. At Nika's Pizza, we focus on four popular styles, each with its own unique characteristics:

Neapolitan: This traditional Italian pizza features a thin, soft crust, topped with simple ingredients like San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil.
It’s typically cooked at high temperatures in a wood-fired oven, resulting in a slightly charred, flavorful crust.

New York Style: Known for its large, foldable slices, New York-style pizza has a crispy yet chewy crust. It’s usually topped with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese, making it a popular choice for a quick meal on the go.

Sicilian: This style is characterized by its thick, rectangular slices and a fluffy crust. Sicilian pizza is often topped with a rich tomato sauce and a variety of cheeses and toppings, making it a hearty option.

Chicago Deep Dish: Unlike traditional pizzas, Chicago deep dish features a thick, buttery crust that acts as a deep pan. It’s layered with cheese, toppings, and chunky tomato sauce, resulting in a filling and satisfying meal.
