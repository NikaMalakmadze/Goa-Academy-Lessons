About Adjara

Adjara is a Georgian region along the Black Sea, renowned for its natural beauty, including stunning beaches and mountains. It is a popular destination for both relaxation and adventure.

**Did you know?** Adjara was once part of the Ottoman Empire before it became part of Georgia. The region’s unique history gives it a blend of Georgian and Ottoman influences.


Famous Places to Visit

Batumi Boulevard

A beautiful coastal promenade in Batumi, lined with gardens, cafes, and historic landmarks. Ideal for strolling or cycling.

Gonio Fortress

A historic Roman fortification located near Batumi. It offers a glimpse into the ancient past and stunning views of the surrounding area.

Culture & Traditions

Adjara has a blend of Georgian and Ottoman cultural influences, reflected in its architecture, cuisine, and music. Visitors can explore local traditions like folk music and dance.

Activities

Enjoy activities such as hiking in the Adjara Mountains, relaxing on the beaches of Batumi, and sampling the region’s wines.
