About Khinkali

Khinkali are traditional Georgian dumplings, filled with minced meat, herbs, and spices. They are a staple in Georgian cuisine and are enjoyed by locals and visitors alike.

**Pro Tip:** When eating Khinkali, remember to hold it by the top knot to avoid spilling the juice inside!

Popular Khinkali Varieties

Meat-filled Khinkali

The most common variety, typically filled with minced beef or pork, flavored with aromatic herbs.

Vegetarian Khinkali

A flavorful alternative, filled with mushrooms, cheese, and spinach.

**Health Tip:** Khinkali can be enjoyed in moderation as part of a balanced diet, but be mindful of the rich filling!
