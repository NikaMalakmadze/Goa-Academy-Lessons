Amazing Lion Facts

Did you know? Lions are the only big cats that live in groups, called prides. A pride is typically made up of related females, their cubs, and a small number of adult males.

  • Lions are the only cats that live in social groups called prides, unlike other big cats.
  • They are primarily nocturnal hunters and rely on teamwork to catch prey.
  • A lion's mane is not just for show—it's a symbol of strength and health.
