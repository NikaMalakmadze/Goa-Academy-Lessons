The Power of Linux

Linux is a free and open-source operating system kernel that powers everything from smartphones to web servers. It's known for its stability, security, and flexibility.

Many popular distributions (like Ubuntu, Fedora, and Debian) make Linux accessible for developers, IT professionals, and hobbyists alike.

Linux Facts

  • Linux was created by Linus Torvalds in 1991.
  • Linux is used by the majority of web servers on the internet.
  • It is highly customizable and can be tailored to suit individual needs.
  • Many major tech companies, like Google, use Linux-based systems.
