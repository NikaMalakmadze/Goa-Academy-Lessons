The Majestic Owl

Owls are known for their ability to hunt in complete darkness. Their silent flight and exceptional vision make them master hunters of the night.

Owls belong to the family Strigidae, and they are found all over the world. They are an important part of many ecosystems, helping to control rodent populations.

Interesting Owl Facts

  • Owls can rotate their heads 270 degrees.
  • Some owls can fly at speeds of up to 40 miles per hour.
  • They are expert hunters, able to hear prey even under snow or soil.
  • Owls have specialized feathers that allow them to fly silently.
