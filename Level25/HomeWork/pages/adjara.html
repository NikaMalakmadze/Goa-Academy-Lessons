Explore the beautiful region of Adjara, a Georgian coastal paradise on the Black Sea.

Discover Adjara

Adjara is known for its breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and rich history. From the stunning Batumi beach to the picturesque mountains, Adjara offers something for every traveler.

Batumi, the capital city, is famous for its modern architecture, botanical gardens, and charming Old Town.

Fun Facts About Adjara

  • Adjara has a subtropical climate, perfect for beach tourism.
  • Batumi is home to one of the oldest botanical gardens in the world.
  • The region boasts diverse flora and fauna, including endemic species.
  • Adjara is rich in ancient folklore and traditions.
